Privacy Policy

At BMS Bites, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information
  • Username and password (encrypted)
  • Order history and preferences
  • Pickup time selections
2. Usage Data
  • Pages visited and features used
  • Order patterns and frequency
  • Device and browser information
  • Session duration and interaction data

How We Use Your Information

  • Order Processing: To facilitate food orders and coordinate with canteen vendors
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and enhance user experience
  • Communication: To send order confirmations and important service updates
  • Analytics: To provide personalized recommendations and improve menu offerings

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Password encryption using secure hashing algorithms
  • Secure database storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited employee access to personal data

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is shared only with:

  • Canteen Vendors: Order details necessary for food preparation
  • College Administration: Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics (if required)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data correction or deletion
  • Opt-out of data collection (may limit service functionality)
  • Export your order history

Cookies and Tracking

We use session cookies to maintain your login state and shopping cart. These cookies are essential for the platform to function and do not track you across other websites.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. Order history is maintained for 12 months for record-keeping purposes. You may request account deletion at any time.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated through the platform. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us through the Contact Us page.
